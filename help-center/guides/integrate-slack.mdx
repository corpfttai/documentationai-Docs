description: Step-by-step on connecting AdaptAI to Slack.
---

## Overview

Deploy AdaptAI answers directly in Slack channels.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to AdaptAI dashboard and click "Integrations".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Slack">
    Click "Connect to Slack" and follow the authorization prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Channels">
    Pick which Slack channels should receive AI support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the Bot">
    In Slack, type `/adaptai help` to confirm connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
