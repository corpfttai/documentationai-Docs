---
title: AI Not Responding
description: Fix AdaptAI chat not replying to users.
---

## Symptoms

- No reply in chat interface
- "Something went wrong" error

## Causes

- Integration disconnected
- Network issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Integration">
    Go to AdaptAI > Integrations. Ensure status is "Connected" for each system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh and Retry">
    Reload chat or clear browser cache, then try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
