## Overview

AdaptAI connects with most HR platforms for seamless employee data sync.

## Steps

1. Open AdaptAI dashboard
2. Go to "Integrations" tab
3. Select your HR provider (e.g., Workday, BambooHR)
4. Click "Connect Account"
5. Authorize access when prompted

## Need Help?
Contact support@adaptai.com for setup assistance.
